Who am I?

I also have other hobbies, one that I really like is Olympic Weightlifting, a sport in which you have to lift a barbell over your head. I've been playing guitar for several years now, but I only play it when I'm bored and when I feel like it.

Education

I completed primary and secondary school in San Agustin School, in Bolivia, and I graduated in 2019. I am currently doing post-secondary studies at Laurentian University, Canada, in the program of Computer Science (Bachelor of Computer Science).

Aspirations

I am still learning and trying to improve my skills at coding because I love doing this, that's why my goal is to land a job as a web developer some day.
